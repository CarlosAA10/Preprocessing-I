Albert Einstein

About

Einstein was a phenomenal genius in his radical approach to seeking answers about the universe. An amazing quality of Einstein was his extreme dedication, which others would see as not normal, in researching his theory of General Relativity. Einstein was so obsessed with his research that he would not hesitate by any means to not miss the eclipses to gather evidence to prove his theory of General Relativity.

Skills

While most of the skills possessed by Einstein were in his understanding of physics and mathematics, he also possessed skills in his field such as:


  • Cohesiveness within a group
  • Attention to detail
  • Project management
  • Creating and keeping deadlines
